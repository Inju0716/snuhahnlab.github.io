Human Factors

Human factors and engineering psychologists study how people interact with machines and technology. They use psychological science to guide the design of products, systems and everyday devices. Many of us experience anxiety the first time we use a new device. Human factors and engineering psychologists apply what they know about human behavior to help businesses design products, systems and devices. They combine technology and psychology to improve our interactions with the systems and equipment we use daily.
